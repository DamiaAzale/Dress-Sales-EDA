# Dress Sales — Exploratory Data Analysis

## About
An exploratory data analysis (EDA) on dress sales data using two combined 
datasets for a more comprehensive understanding of customer preferences, 
marketing strategies, and product development. The analysis explores how 
different dress attributes impact sales performance.

## Objectives
- Identify key factors that drive dress sales
- Uncover hidden patterns and trends in sales data
- Predict future sales performance based on trends and correlations
- Optimize pricing strategies based on customer behaviour

## Key Insights
- Analysed popular dress styles, price ranges and seasonal trends
- Explored the relationship between dress attributes and sales performance
- Examined factors such as fashion trends, seasonal influences and 
  economic conditions
